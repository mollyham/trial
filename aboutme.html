About Me

Hi, I am Sandy

I have been fascinated by Human Rights from a young age. The ability to work with others in the quest for a better just world is one of the most useful and essential skills one can develop in school and college. I also believe that Human Rights are at the core of humanity. The notion that man is born free and deserves to live in a free world moves me miles. Any unjust move against humanity in whichever form it manifests is ungodly. I always strive to improve my skills and develop innovative and more effective strategies on how to better the world.

I always knew that I wanted to become a Human Rights Lawyer and Activist. I have a bachelor's degree in Laws. I have more than 10 years of experience working with marginalized groups and other sections of society fighting for Human Rights. I subscribe to various Human Rights related Organizations. I look forward to working with other human rights defenders including Organizations
